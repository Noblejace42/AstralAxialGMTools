About Weakest Link

Weakest Link is a student-driven nonprofit incubated at Worcester Polytechnic Institute. We harness blockchain technology to connect everyday donors with entrepreneurs in underbanked regions. Our mission is simple: strengthen every link in the global economic chain by making capital transparent, affordable, and community-powered. Every surplus dollar is reinvested into long-term infrastructure like clean energy grids and digital marketplaces—compounding impact beyond the first loan cycle.
