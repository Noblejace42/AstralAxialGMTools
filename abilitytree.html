Classless RPG Abilities

Ability Name Move Type Recharge/Type Ability Description
Ace up the Sleeve passive If you would roll to overcome, dazzle swindle haggle, or resist peril and you do not add a single Talent die to your dice pool, step up a die in your pool.
Ad Astra Per Aspera light encounter If you would take a wound, you may instead decrease a Talent by 1.
Ask Questions Later passive recovery If you initiate combat with a creature you were meant to interrogate, question, or speak with, you may ask the GM one of the following questions at the end of combat and receive a useful answer:
Why was this creature here?
What did this creature want?
Why did this creature attack us?
Whom does this creature serve?
Apothecary passive When you journey planetside you may contest Awareness|Recall, on a success, you may gain 2 Bandages I1d6 (Special, Consumable 1) or 2 Rations (Special, Consumable 1).
Black Market Connections passive When you arrive at a settlement, if it has a criminal underground, you may ask the GM one of the following questions and get a truthful if incomplete answer:
Who runs the criminal underworld here?
What is the main illegal trade?
How dangerous is the criminal underworld?
Would I be able to find [item] at the black market here?
Blot out the Sun heavy encounter Attack using a far-range or long-range weapon then you may expend an ammo to attack again.
Blood for the Blood God light encounter When you kill an enemy with a (Melee), (Brutal), or (Violent) weapon you may reduce your Strain by 1.
Breaker of War medium encounter You may attempt to defuse a hostile situation, contest Presence|Charm. On a success, the GM will choose one of the following outcomes:
Confusion is sown among the enemy
Discontent stirs among the enemy
Defection occurs within the enemy
The enemy stands down, albeit with some unease
Increase your Luck when one of these occurs.
Bullet Time medium recovery Until your next turn you cannot be the target of an attack. Additionally, if you resist peril during this time, step up every die in your dice pool.
Bulwark medium encounter Declare a creature in the same zone as you. Until the end of the encounter, they may add your armor dice to their defense as long as they are in the same zone as you. When they take damage, you can choose to take half of that damage for them, this damage cannot be reduced.
Called Shot light encounter When you succeed an attack with a weapon that has the near-range, long-range, or far-range tags, you may choose 2 of the following:
You instantly kill the creature if it has less than 10 health.
You may knock a weapon or object out of the targets hand.
You can inflict a random wound on the target.
You regain usage of this ability, increase your Strain die.
Cheap Shot heavy encounter Attack a creature, stepping up 1 die of your choice. If you succeed, choose 2 of the following:
Roll 2d6 and add it to your damage total.
Gain a surge.
Move to an adjacent zone.
Knock the target backwards 10 feet.
(Melee only) Pilfer an item not being worn or held in the hands of the opponent.
Cheat Death passive recovery The next time you would die, you do not, instead suffer a Strain break and set your Strain die to 8. Reset your health to its maximum.
Charming and Open light encounter When you speak frankly with someone, you can ask them a question from the list below. They must answer it truthfully, then they may ask you a question from the list (which you must answer truthfully).
Whom do you serve?
What do you wish I would do?
How can I get you to _____?
What are you really feeling right now?
What do you most desire?
Companion passive Your companion is _____.
Robotic, Organic
Your companion has 1d4 in all skills. Additionally, they are ____ (choose 2).
Crafty: Increase Tech and Precision by 2
Strong: Increase Might and Presence by 2
Fast: Increase Awareness and Speed by 2
Tough: Increase Fortitude and Will by 2
(robotic) Technical: Increase Tech by 3
If your companion is nearby and aiding you, gain the following ability:
When you contest or attack you may add 1 applicable dice from your companions Skills to your dice pool.
Dark Confidant passive You may swap a die of the same size in your dice pool and your opposition's dice pool (any contested roll or GM CR roll) after seeing the results of the roll. When you do this, increase your Strain.
Devilish Po heavy recovery On your next turn you may take 3 heavy moves instead of 1 heavy or 2 medium moves.
Discombobulate light encounter Add your Recall and Presence to your dice pool the next time you damage with a melee weapon. You cannot incur complications on this roll.
Dumb Luck light recovery If you successfully resist peril, you may gain a surge and regain use of this ability. Alternatively, you may increase your Luck by 1.
Engage! engineer engineer encounter You can activate an axial instantaneously, randomly giving 1d4 hardpoint modules on your ship the (Damaged).

Forth Eorlingas! passive recovery If you initiate combat while outnumbered, increase your Luck. Choose another player, they increase their Luck. Choose a different player, they gain a surge. Choose a different player, they double their initiative score for this combat.
Flash Out heavy encounter If you attack with an (Explosive) weapon against an unsuspecting, surprised, or incapacitated target, you may step up all dice in your pool, on a success, step up all dice in your damage pool as well.
Flames Of My Desire passive Choose one of the following desires:
Pure destruction
Power over others
Conquest
Riches aplenty
Fame and glory
Esoteric Knowledge
Whenever you contest or attack in pursuit of your desire, you may step up the largest and smallest dice in your pool. If the smallest die rolls higher than the highest die, the DM may introduce a complication as a result of your heedless pursuit.
Focused Training passive When you spend a period of more than a week resting, you may decrease up to 2 Skills. For each Skill decreased this way, increase a Skill by 1.
Font of Knowledge passive If you ask a question or seek knowledge related to a Talent you have, you can choose to have the GM give you a useful or relevant piece of information, if you do, increase your Strain die.
Fool me Once passive When you fail your second contest or attack in a given encounter, you may instead succeed.
Frantic Parry light encounter When you would be the target of a damage, you may instead lose no health. Increase your Strain by 1.
Gatecrasher light encounter When you contest Might|Skill to destroy an inanimate obstacle you may step up your Might die. Additionally on a success, choose 2 of the following:
It doesn’t take a very long time
It doesn’t introduce any complications
It doesn’t make an inordinate amount of noise
You can fix the thing without significant effort
Give me Liberty! passive recovery If you die in defense of your friends, innocents, or a noble cause, everyone in your party increases their Luck. They all gain a surge. Your next character may start with a connection to this character and may possess an item your character died with. The GM will determine how your character acquired this item.
Glancing Blow passive You may treat all armor you wear as if it has (Protective).
Heavy on the Saddle passive You can exceed your mass capacity by up to 50 before suffering negative consequences. You may step up 2 dice in your dice pool if you are assigned a “trailblazer” role by a move.
Herald of Law light encounter Declare that a creature is breaking the law, step up your Presence and Charm and contest Presence|Charm. On a success, the GM will choose one of the following:
The creature will flee you to avoid attention.
The creature will become hostile towards you, step up 2 dice next time you attack that creature.
The creature will beg and plead, step up 2 dice when you next dazzle, swindle, haggle with that creature.
Hondo’s Return passive Whenever you return to an outpost of civilization in triumph, you may step up your Presence and contest Presence|Charm or Presence|Might. On a success, you impress anyone who sees your return, step up a die in your dice pool the next time you dazzle, swindle, haggle with them.
Hotshot pilot move pilot move recovery Take two pilot moves. Increase your Strain by 1.
I Am Vengeance heavy encounter Attack a target in the same zone as you. Treat your weapon as if it is (Brutal) and (Violent 3) for this attack.
Indomitable light recovery If you would fail an attempt to overcome, you succeed instead.
Iron-Willed light recovery When your Strain is 10 and it would be increased beyond that, you may instead reduce your Strain die by 1d4. You can choose to postpone the effect of a Strain break until your next recovery.
Jack Of All Trades passive Whenever you gain a new Talent you may start it at 1d6 instead of 1d4. When you first gain this ability, learn a new Talent.
Journey Before Destination passive When you journey planetside you may write a new bond with one of your companions. Reduce your Strain by 1 when you journey among friends whom you share bonds with.
Juggernaut passive If every worn armor slot is filled with (Heavy) armor or armor with A1d10 or better as its dice, you may step up all armor dice when you roll defense.
Kingly Wit passive When you dazzle, swindle, haggle with a creature you have a bond with, step up 1 die in your dice pool for each bond you have with that creature. If you suffer a complication while you dazzle, swindle, haggle with a creature, the GM might give you a bond with that creature.
Lucky Tool passive You have a special tool that carries you through your journey. The tool is _____.
Old and beat up
Kept clean and shiny
Well worn
Uniquely decorated
Describe the type of tool you have and what it is used for, the tool has a 1d8 die and is (Lucky). If you would step up a die in your dice pool while using this tool, step up two dice.
Melee Master passive Whenever you use (Melee) weapon, you may treat it as if it has the (Potent 1). If it already has (Potent X), it now has (Potent X+1). You cannot drop or be disarmed of your (Melee) weapons against your will so long as you are conscious.
Master Quartermaster passive Whenever you contest to help set up camp, manage logistics, or cook you may step up 2 dice in your pool. You cannot incur complications when doing these tasks.
May The Force Be With You passive encounter You may add your Luck when you dogfight or open fire. Step up a die in your pool.
Max Burn passive encounter You may expend a fuel to kick your ship's engine into overdrive, doubling your speed modifier until the beginning of your next turn. This can be activated during the merge at the beginning of combat to increase your starting advantage instead.
Port in the Storm passive Whenever you return to a place you have been before, and you are well regarded, choose 2 of the following:
You learn how it has changed since you were last here.
You are able to make contact with a useful NPC.
You discover a local rumor.
You are able to get an audience with a local ruler.
Punch God medium recovery If you stand defiant in the face of overwhelming odds, existential terror, or a creature far beyond your skills, you may step up all the dice in your dice pool and attack or overcome in opposition to this force. You cannot incur complications on this roll.
Push beyond limit light encounter You may step up 2 dice in your dice pool. If you do this, increase your Strain by 1.
Pause Menu passive All weapons have the (Throwable) tag.
Rigid Focus light encounter When you roll a dice pool that includes a Talent at 1d12, you may reroll up to 2 dice and use the new results to find your total.
Rampant Hobbyist passive When you Bolster and Carouse you may forgo the benefit to gain a new Talent (1d4).
Rage Against the Machine medium encounter You have mastered putting yourself into a state of murderous rage. You may step all dice in your pool for the remainder of the encounter. At the end of the encounter, increase your Strain die by 2.
Resist and Bite light encounter When you are dealt damage by a melee weapon you may choose to instead take no damage and may deal up to half that damage to a target within your zone.
Rip And Tear heavy encounter You may attack a target, if you kill them with your subsequent damage, attack another target. Repeat this until you do not kill a target.
Sage Knight passive If you spend an hour or more in quiet meditation after a battle, you may decrease your Strain. If your Strain is 0, increase your luck.
Second Wind: medium recovery You may recuperate.
Setup Strike medium encounter Attack an enemy in the same zone as an ally of yours. Step up 2 dice in the pool of the next attack made by an ally in that zone.
Sheer Spite light encounter When you make a dice pool roll, roll your Will die and add it to the total.
Shoot First passive recovery You may choose to have your turn at the top of the turn order when you initiate combat, even if surprised.
Sic Parvis Magna pilot move recovery Increase your advantage by 1d4. You may open fire on the lowest advantage starship.
Steal the Moon passive When you take this ability, gain a Talent called “heist planning” at expertise 1d8. When you contest to plot a heist, step up a Skill die and “heist planning” . On a success, the GM will give you an additional relevant and useful piece of information relating to the heist you are planning.
Strafe Lurch positioning recovery Move to a zone that is exactly 3 zones away from you. You may attack an enemy in this zone immediately.
Strider passive recovery When you journey planetside or make camp, step up all dice in your next dice pool. Rolls in this pool cannot incur complications.

Seeing Red passive When you deal damage, you may increase your Strain. You may add your Strain to your damage total.

Tactical Reload passive If you take an hour or more to examine your weapon, you may choose a tag it has to remove. If you remove a tag in this way, the GM will give you two options for tags. You may add one of the options to your weapon.

Volta Gloria passive recovery If you have no surges and you initiate combat, gain a surge.
